Tagging text is the task of assigning a part-of-speech tag for each of the tokens in a sentence. For example, suppose we had the sentence: "В том институте все уехали.", we would first tokenise it using our tokeniser: 

# sent_id = 1
# text = В том институте все уехали.
1	В	_	_	_	_	_	_	_	_
2	том	_	_	_	_	_	_	_	_
3	институте	_	_	_	_	_	_	_	_
4	все	_	_	_	_	_	_	_	_
5	уехали	_	_	_	_	_	_	_	_
6	.	_	_	_	_	_	_	_	_

Then we would go through the text line by line, and give each line a part-of-speech tag in the fourth column. The part-of-speech tags are from the Universal Dependencies project and can be found with full definitions here.
